We Bring History to Life

Based in Hartford Connecticut, Imposters Impersonating History impersonates many of New England’s historical notables from the Victorian and Edwardian periods. From entertaining your dinner guests to giving a historic tour, we are your best source for providing historical interactive entertainment. Whether we are portraying one of our favorite characters or a character you would like us to research, WE BRING HISTORY TO LIFE! By exposing the personalities of these unique individuals we provide an intimate view into local history. Properly attired and in character, spending time with the Imposters is always entertaining and educational.

As Charles Pond spelled out in his bequest: "I give to the City of Hartford, this gift of about ninety (90) acres, to be forever held and used as a public park.
